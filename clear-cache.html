Clear Cache & Service Worker

Instructions:
1. Click "Clear All Caches" to remove all cached data
2. Click "Unregister Service Worker" to disable caching
3. Click "Force Service Worker Update" to update the Service Worker
4. Refresh the page and test your application
5. Close this tab and return to your app
Manual Steps:
• Press Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to hard refresh
• Open DevTools → Application → Storage → Clear All
• Or use Incognito/Private browsing mode
